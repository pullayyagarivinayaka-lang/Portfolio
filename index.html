About

A little about how I work

I'm Vinayaka, a Computer Science Engineering (Data Science) undergraduate based in Hyderabad. I like taking a folder of raw CSVs and turning it into something a non-technical stakeholder can read in ten seconds — a KPI card, a clean chart, a dashboard that answers the question before it's asked.

My recent work spans two public Tableau dashboards covering more than 28,000 combined records, a from-scratch career guidance website, and internships in both data analysis and web development. I care about clean data as much as clean design — half the job is asking the dataset the right questions before you visualize anything.
